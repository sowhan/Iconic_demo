About Us

More than a salon.
A legacy since 2006.

"Transform every client into their best version."

Iconic was founded 20 years ago by Mrs. Suma Sridhar and Mrs. Geetha Mallya and has been through various hardships to be what it is now. Our motto is to transform every client into their best version. We make sure to deliver customized quality service to each of our clients according to their lifestyle and budget without compromising the quality. We have loyal clients who have been coming for chemical hair treatments for over 10 years with consistently positive feedback — a testament to the trust we've built. With thousands of happy clients, "We can fix any type of hair/skin problem."
